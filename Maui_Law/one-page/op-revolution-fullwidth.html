Our Attorneys

Lance Hevizy

Partner

Mr. Hevizy practices in the areas of business litigation, real estate law and personal injury.

Business litigation cases involving contract disputes, business fraud, breach of fiduciary duty, mergers and acquisitions, corporate liability, intellectual property, conversion of property and shareholder derivative lawsuits.

Real Estate area of practice includes drafting and reviewing real estate contracts, residential and commercial leases, transfer of title, lease with option to purchase, foreclosures and landlord/tenant relations.

Mr. Hevizy is committed to representing individuals who have suffered injuries involving transportation accidents, premises liability, dangerous products, or the negligence of others.

"Our overall success includes adaptive, innovative strategies built to win as well as the effective utilization of cutting-edge technology," says Mr. Hevizy. "From the very start of litigation, Hevizy and Mallios makes a point to set the pace so that our side has absolute command of the facts and issues at play. Preparation for trial is key for success. Many trials are won or lost before the lawyers even enter the courtroom." Hevizy & Mallios understands this and provides formidable resources and cutting-edge trial techniques to help clients succeed in court.

Partner

We look forward to demonstrating our skill, expertise and integrity should you ever need legal advice or representation.
